About Me

Hello! I’m Apu Das — a passionate Competitive Programmer, Problem Setter, and former trainer at AUST ACM Lab 2. I have a strong interest in AI and the creative intersection of technology and problem-solving.

I am honored to be a three-time Intra-AUST Programming Contest Champion, and hold the record for the highest national contest participation (17 contests) by any individual from AUST.

I also served as the Vice President of the AUST Programming and Informatics Club, where I led the organization of the national programming contest AUST IUPC 2025.

Outside the competitive scene, I enjoy game development, photography, and exploring the wonders of the universe through astronomy. I’m constantly driven by new challenges and expanding my knowledge across diverse fields.
